Definition: Conditional Probability

Let \(A\) and \(B\) be events in the sample space \(S\), with \(P(B)\neq 0\). The conditional probability of \(A\) given \(B\) is

\[P(A|B)=\frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}\]
